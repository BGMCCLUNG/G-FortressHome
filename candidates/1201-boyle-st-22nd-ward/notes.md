## Why This Property Is Extraordinary

This is not a standard North Side rowhouse. Key distinguishing features visible from Street View:

**The facade:** Front clad in cut limestone / dressed ashlar stone — cream/tan layered coursework. Premium construction material rarely seen on distressed properties in this price range. Red brick on side and rear elevations. A building built with architectural intention and presence.

**The roofline:** Mansard-style roof with decorative pediment dormer — arched window flanked by classical molding, fish-scale slate shingles on the roof face, corbeled brick detail at the cornice line. Reference 1 DNA at the roofline. The ornament is damaged but present and largely intact.

**The cornice:** Decorative brick corbeling between 2nd and 3rd floors — visible and largely intact. The building was designed with presence, not utility.

**The scale:** 3,090 sq ft across 5 bedrooms in a 3-story structure. Substantial for a North Side rowhouse. Vertical presence on the block.
